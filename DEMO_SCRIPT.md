# Riskseer Demo Script

## Opening

Riskseer catches when work still looks normal, but the support for continuing is weak.

Most excavation incidents do not start with obvious failure. They happen when people continue under partial, stale, or conflicting information.

So instead of just flagging violations, Riskseer evaluates whether continuing work is actually supported by the available evidence.

## How To Frame The Product

It pulls in tickets, activity, assets, and field signals, groups them into evolving cases, and evaluates each case as a real operational situation.

From there, it answers three things:

1. What is happening
2. What is weak
3. What should happen next

## Short Answers

### How is this different from existing tools?

Most systems track compliance or data. Riskseer evaluates decision quality, especially when everything still looks normal.

### Who uses this?

Operators, supervisors, and anyone responsible for deciding whether work continues.

### Is it assigning blame?

No. It is not about blame. It is about whether the decision is supported by evidence.

### What makes this defensible?

It keeps the reasoning inspectable. You can see what was observed, what was derived, what is weak, and why the posture changed.
